Page 2: Instructions
At the top of the screen are two rows of boxes. Each box in the top row has a unique design inside. Each box in the bottom row has a unique number inside. Notice that each box with a design inside can be paired with a box with a number inside. During this test a new pair of boxes will appear in the middle of the screen. Your job is to decide if the design and number in this new pair match one of the pairs you see above.

If the new pair matches one of the pairs above, press the "SAME" button. If the new pair does not match one of the pairs above, press the "DIFFERENT" button.
